The Blissless EP is a collaboration between NCB and J-Rey. It mainly focuses on Hip Hop, but ranges from electro-psychedelic to warm acoustic indie. There are playful and poignant moments alike, featuring tracks such as "Orange" and "Crunk Dust," which are both credited as kicking off the burst of folk-tech recordings that followed for several years.
